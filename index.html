The Morphism Diagnostic Framework

Four material paradigms, one unified compiler. Each morphism represents a distinct tactile language translated into deterministic machine instruction.

Glassmorphism
Maximizes visibility for precise preview panels and laser toolpaths. Frosted clarity with depth.
Woodmorphism
Warm, organic variation and depth. Natural grain patterns inform tactile feedback.
Papermorphism
Stackable, annotatable layers of fabric design. Fold, crease, annotate.
Siliconmorphism
Industrial precision and chemical mixing. The substrate of computation.

The 8-Stage Compilation Pipeline

From creative intent to physical dispatch — every stage is versioned, reversible, and mathematically provable.

1

Intent Capture

Natural language or SVG parsed into AST. Every decision logged as semantic node.

parse(intent) → AST
2

Orchestration

Chief Spatial Architect distributes work. Multi-threaded with dependency graph resolution.

orchestrate(AST) → DAG
3

Physics

Rust XPBD solver: soft-body kinematics, cloth draping, thread tension.

solve(mesh, constraints)
4

Render

WebGPU procedural textures — wood grain, fabric weave, leather grain.

render(PBR, noise)
5

Synesthesia

Audio-haptic mapping: visual data into vibration envelopes and spectral soundscapes.

map(visual → haptic)
6

Geometry Unwrap

ABF++ flattening: 3D surfaces to 2D toolpaths with minimal distortion.

unwrap(mesh) → UV
7

Fabrication Export

G-Code, DST, PNG generation with trapping, color separation, registration marks.

export(toolpath, format)
8

Cyber-Physical Handshake

Containerized dispatch via MQTT/OPC-UA. Real-time telemetry feedback loop.

dispatch(machine, verify)
